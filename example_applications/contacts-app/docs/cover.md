# Rolodex

> Your contacts, connected

A modern contact management library for Node.js applications. Rolodex stores people, organizations, and relationships with a flexible schema and full-text search.

- **People & Orgs** — Manage individuals and organizations in one place
- **Relationships** — Link contacts to each other with typed associations
- **Custom Fields** — Extend contact records with your own metadata
- **Search** — Full-text search across names, emails, notes, and custom fields
